How much does a motorcycle shipping cost?

The average cost of shipping a motorcycle is between $350 to $700 for the approximate distance of 1000 miles. For the shipments under 1000 miles, we gave motorcycle shipping price that ranges between $180 to $300. Other than that, the cost depends on these various factors:

  • Distance
  • Make and model
  • Type of shipping
  • Extra coverage
  • Residential fees

Time in transporting a motorcycle across the country.

The time of transportation depends on the location where you want to ship your vehicles and the route that has been chosen; generally, it takes 15-30 business days to ship the motorcycle. If you want your motorcycle shipping on an urgent basis, then we have the option of expedited shipping service.

How do you prepare your motorcycle for shipping?

Follow the tips to prepare your motorcycle for shipping with the Auto Transportation Center.

  • Fill the tank only 1/4 of its capacity.
  • Charge the battery as an alternate option.
  • Clean your motorcycle.
  • Properly filled and inflated tires.
  • Check fluid leaks.
  • Steering is unlocked
  • Fold all the mirrors.
