How do you prepare your vehicle for snowbird auto shipping?

While we are taking the responsibility of taking care of your vehicle during the whole transportation process, it is your responsibility to prepare your vehicle for shipping. Let's see how you can prepare your vehicle for Snowbird auto shipping.

Wash your vehicle thoroughly.

To get a clear look at your car, wash it thoroughly, which will help you mark all the pre-existing damages to the vehicle. And don't forget to take pictures from all sides if in case some damage happens during the transportation.

Remove all the loose parts.

We highly recommend you remove all the loose items attached to your vehicle. This can cause problems in transportation.

Remove all the personal stuff.

Remember not to leave any personnel stuff in your vehicle, like charging cables or water bottles in your vehicle. If you want to ship some personal items, we recommend you put them in the trunk with a maximum weight of 100 pounds.

Carry out the necessary maintenance.

Make sure all the tires are fully inflated and have a fully charged car battery.

Limit the tank fuel.

The tank should be filled with 1/8 or 1/4 of its full capacity.
