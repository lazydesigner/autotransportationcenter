Types of Open Car Shipping

There can be some differences between open car transport and enclosed car transport.

A Single car

.

Single-car transport refers to a trailer that can ship only one vehicle at a time. During the whole transportation process, your vehicle will be secured and left in its designated location in the trailer.

This method of auto transportation eliminates the need to wait for the delivery of other vehicles. Still, this method is quite expensive because you have to pay to ship a single vehicle rather than splitting the cost with other passengers. This eliminates the option of cost-sharing with other customers.

Only one tier

Your vehicle will travel with other vehicles as well, as single-level open car shipping can accommodate many vehicles at once.

It is still the quickest option for auto transport service because in this you are required to interact with other consumers.

Multi-level

Multiple-level open carriers are frequently used for auto transport services because they can contain more vehicles in a single trip.

They are more affordable and simple to schedule. But as they carry the majority of vehicles this results in delay in delivery.

How do you know when your vehicle is ready for transportation?

Before the carrier driver arrives to pick up your vehicle, it is important to prepare the vehicle for its journey. Following are some tips that you should follow.

Rinse it

Wash your vehicle thoroughly before it's shipping; it will give you a clear look to examine your vehicle for any existing damage.

Turn off all the alarms.

You should just turn off all your alarms that are pre-installed in your vehicle.

Check for any air leakage and pressure.

Air leakage can create a big problem for your vehicle's tires if they are not properly inflated.

How do you prepare your vehicle for Open car shipping?

To prepare your vehicle for auto transportation service, you need to follow the following steps:

  • Disable all alarm systems that are installed in your vehicle.
  • Remove all the unattached components.
  • Remove all fragile accessories.
  • Wash your vehicle for clear visibility of damages.
  • Give the carrier a set of spare keys.
  • If you give standard service, you can't put more than 100 pounds of personal stuff in your vehicle.
