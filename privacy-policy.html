Privacy Policy

The Auto Transportation Center is a website that works as a data controller for your personal data.

We have set up our privacy policy, which shows how we process data that is gathered by Auto Transportation Center and explains why we need to collect some personal information about you. As a result, you must read our privacy policy before hiring us as your auto shippers. We promise to keep your personal details safe and confidential.

Minors

This website is not intended for minors, and we do not collect or store personal information from someone who is under the age of 13. PLEASE DO NOT USE THIS SITE OR ANY OF OUR SERVICES IF YOU ARE UNDER 13 OR YOUNGER.

Personal information we collect:

When you visit the Auto Transportation Center, we automatically gather some information and data about your device, such as your time zone, web browser, IP address and some other cookies that are present in your device. Furthermore, as you navigate the site, we also gather information about the web pages you visit, the terms and websites that suggest our website, and how you interact with the site. This data is called "device information" which was gathered automatically. To fulfil the agreement, we may collect your personal details that were submitted by you for registration like name, surname, address etc.

Why do we process your data?

Customer security is our first priority, so we only gather that much information that is necessary to maintain the site. The automatically gathered information is generally used to identify the potential status of abuse and compile statistical data on website usage. You can visit us without any hesitation or disclosing any information about yourself. If you want to use some features of our website or submit other information like your personal data such as name, surname, email, city etc. You can simply refuse to provide any kind of personal data but as a result, you may not be able to use some features of our website like the newsletter or contact us directly through the website, for example.

Your rights:

If you want to use the following privileges, quickly contact us. And please be aware that we are gathering your personal information to fulfil any contract with European residents in future. Links to other websites:

We may contain some other links on our website that we do not own or manage. Please keep in mind that we don't have any control over the privacy policy adopted by the third-party websites. We advise you to read their privacy policy before leaving our website in case they are collecting your personal data.

Information security:

We protect the data that you have provided to us by storing it on computer servers in a controlled and safe way that is free from unauthorized access or disclosure. To prevent unauthorized access, modification and disclosure of personal information we maintain reasonable administrative, technical and physical precautions. Data that is transmitted over the internet or a wireless network cannot be assured.

Legal disclosure:

If it is required or permitted by the law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, and when we believe that disclosure is important to protect our rights, investigate fraud, or fulfil a government request, we may disclose the information that we collected, used or received.
