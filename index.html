How Car Shipment Cost is Calculated

Weight and size of vehicles

Vehicle size and Weight play an important role in determining the cost to ship a car . Smaller cars are usually lighter and smaller and take less space in the trailer. Bigger cars (SUVs, Commercial trucks) are heavier and bigger in size consuming more space. Ultimately Affecting the cost.

Type of car shipping Service

If you go with an open car shipping service, it will be going to cost you less . but if you opt for the option of Enclosed car shipping Service, it will Charge You an extra 60% Than the open Car Shipping Service. Note( enclosed provides extra safety and protection to your car).

Shipping Distance

If your vehicle is well maintained and running, it makes the process easy hence decreasing the cost. But if your vehicle is non-operable then it Needs Extra help making the process difficult and increasing the cost.

Vehicle Condition

If the Vehicle is in running condition than it would be easy to transport but if car is in non running state there are other tools needed thus cost increses

Final shipping Cost

Your Final shipping cost depends on the above factor, And it Includes all the taxes and insurance of auto shipping covered under federal law. You Can Calculate Your Shipping Cost on the Cost calculator Mentioned on our website.

Car shipping costs are determined by various factors like distance, time, method of shipping, type of vehicle and many more. All these factors will determine the total cost of transporting a vehicle from one place to another.

We accept all major credit cards, postal money orders, certified cheques for the deposit or the full pre-payment of the vehicle.

The bill of lading is very important to record any pre-existing damage on the vehicle during the pickup and delivery. It also serves as an official agreement for car shipping. To file any insurance claim you will need the bill of lading.

Mostly the delivery time is based on the distance that is going to be shipped. Usually, it takes 2-3 days to cover 1000 miles

You can transport personal stuff in your vehicle as long as it doesn't exceed the weight of 100 pounds. Please note that personal stuff is not covered under insurance if it gets missing or damaged.
